Common Questions

World Cup
Transportation FAQs

Quick answers to what most groups ask before booking.

How many passengers can you accommodate? ▼
We can transport groups of up to 10 passengers. A Chevy Suburban works for groups of 1–5, and our 10-passenger van handles groups of 6–10. Just let us know your group size when you request a quote.
Do you offer airport pickups? ▼
Yes, we offer private airport transportation for groups arriving in Kansas City. We track your flight, meet your group at KCI, and get you to your hotel, Airbnb, or wherever you're headed — no waiting on a rideshare surge.
Can we schedule a ride to and from the match? ▼
Yes, pre-arranged stadium transportation is available. We'll get your group to the stadium before kickoff and have a pickup ready when the match ends. We know the area, so we can navigate the post-game traffic patterns.
Do you pick up from hotels or Airbnbs? ▼
Yes, we provide private transportation for visitors staying throughout the Kansas City area — downtown hotels, the Plaza area, Airbnbs, short-term rentals, and beyond. Just provide your address when you request a quote.
Should we book ahead for World Cup transportation? ▼
Yes. Demand will be significantly higher during the event, and match day availability fills up first. If you already know your travel dates, booking early is the best way to guarantee your group has a ride locked in.
How does payment work? ▼
We collect a 25% deposit to hold your reservation. The remaining balance is due on ride day. Deposits are processed securely online and we'll send you a confirmation once your booking is locked in.
What if my group size changes? ▼
No problem. Just let us know ahead of time and we'll adjust the vehicle if needed. The Suburban handles up to 5 passengers, and the van handles up to 10. If your group grows, we'll make sure you have the right ride.
